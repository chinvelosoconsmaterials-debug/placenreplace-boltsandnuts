The Airflow Comfort Funnel.

Every click flows through a guided journey — from quick online curiosity to a tuned system, membership plan, and ongoing service reminders.

  1. Online Ad / Search — Homeowners discover Airflow through search, social, or local partner sites.
  2. Comfort Landing Page — Visitors see clear system options, FAQs, and a savings estimator.
